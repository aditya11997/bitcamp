We Empower Women through Job Creation,promote learning and make women Independent



  1. Increasing savings and Income:
    2. 63% of women increased their savings or income after joining savings cooperatives at our Centers in Chhatisgarh. Savings cooperatives allow women to invest money and then take turns receiving micro-loans to start micro-businesses or invest in education for their children.
  2. Teaching women to read:
    3. If you are illiterate, simple things like reading signs on a road, numbers on a phone, or directions on a medicine bottle make daily life a struggle. READ Centers teach thousands of women to read each year.
  3. Creating Women Leaders
    4. Women rarely hold public leadership roles. READ Centers provide leadership training for women to increase their presence in the public sphere. 61-65% of women report that they are now able to express their opinions in public or in their home. Women report joining committees, facilitating meetings, participating in protests, raising their voices against violence, and organizing community programs.
