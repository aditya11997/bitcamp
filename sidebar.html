Our empowering initiatives undertaken in 2017

Teaching rural women to read and write

If you are illiterate, simple things like reading signs on a road, numbers on a phone, or directions on a medicine bottle make daily life a struggle. Our Centers have dedicated voluntiers who teach thousands of women to read each year.

Organizing Self Help Groups

We have helped women make bring out their ideas and suggestions in front of common people by being a mentor to the common women.We beleive that a healthy community is a key ingredient to women empowerment
Currently Active Self Group activities under our mentorship:
  1. READ- Promotes Reading and Writing skills in native as well as english language
  2. Child Education
  3. Cattle Loan Distribution

0% Interest Loans

We tend to provide 0% Interest loans for extremely needy and people from Below-Powerty-Line(BPL).We would act as a security for the loan provided by the bank.In return,we would encourage entrepreneurship and job creation.Nothing would be charged until they start earning.Once,they start earning,we would take a small chunk of their income towards meeting the loan
